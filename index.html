I’m a product manager with deep roots in FinTech — at Fidelity Investments and Global Payments, I spent those years as the person users called when the product broke.

That experience gave me something most PMs don’t have: I know exactly where users get stuck, why they churn, and what a single friction point actually costs. Now I build products designed around those failure points — before they become someone else’s support ticket.
